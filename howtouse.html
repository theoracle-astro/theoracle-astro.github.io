How to Use

1. Login In: First enter the username and password provided to login to the Oracle tool Here. You will be directed straight to the Oracle main page in a new window.

2. Live GCN At the top of the page you will see Live LIGO/Virgo GCN notices, auto updating with the latest event larger then the rest. From this table you can download the skymap fits or go straight to the GraceID page.

3. Facilities Below the live GCN notices you will see a list of all telescopes which will be contributing to OzGrav EM follow up, many of these have been awarded specific ToO time allowances for EM followup during semester 19A.

4. Update Facility - To submit a facility update either click on the orange button on the Oracle tool page, or select 'Update Facility' from the menu. You will be directed to select a facility. We advised you leave this facility page open constantly for ease of use during your observations. On your facility page you can submit your dates in seconds via google form, and press 'Sumbit another response' to refresh the form after each entry. These updates will be added automatically to the top your facility box on the Oracle tool, for others to follow.

We currenty have 14 facilities that are expected to contribute to O3 EM follow up, ranging from Optical, Infrared to Radio wavelengths.
